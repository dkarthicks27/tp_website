The idea of TECH@PARK was not born in any boardroom discussions. We are not backed by any funding houses. We are a group of like-minded techies who commit our time and effort to give back to the society for a larger social cause. We do not have classrooms or paid instructors. But still, we have made a difference in the quality of talents we produce. Our results are different because our method itself is different.

  • Picking a Candidate

    We identify potential candidates through trusted sources ( i.e. Colleagues/Ex-Students) who know their potential personally. We then interview certain parameters to see if he has 'IT' in him.

  • Team Farming

    Students are expected to stay with the trainer. We encourage them to farm as a team to learn the Engineering process. He will then be involved in real-world projects which can either be Open Source projects or from a potential hirer.

  • Pro-gramming

    Students are trained on the latest market trends & technologies by industry experts. There is no predefined training duration period. It goes on until he or the trainer feels 'COMPLETE'. While joining, our students may not be the same. But they will achieve it when they 'COMP(L)ETE'

  • Placement

    We reach out to companies that need relevant skill sets. As a principle, we never recommend people. We just refer. When both the company and student find themselves matching, then they pursue. Our student becomes ALUMNI and a contributor in the course of action and stay connected with us ever after, as our network grows stronger and wider:)
